About Us

Kasama in Filipino/Tagalog means "together," "in company with," and "being included." Based in Austin, TX, Kasama Tastings is a woman-owned and operated private wine tasting company that offers educational and inclusive tasting experiences for all interested in the world of wine.

Driven by our passion for learning and connection, we provide accessible excellence through curated in-person wine or non-alcoholic wine tastings for all types of drinkers and wine aficionados. Perfect for birthdays, bachelorette parties, corporate events and more.

No matter what your relationship is with wine or alcohol, Kasama Tastings is an inclusive journey to find your palate. Ultimately, you want to love the wine you're with.

My wine journey started as a curious student at UVA (Go Hoos!) exploring the beautiful wineries of Charlottesville, VA. Back then, every sip tasted like... well, grapes. But I was hooked on the experience!
